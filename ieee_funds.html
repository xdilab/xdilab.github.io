IEEE NC East — Conference Attendance Funding (Computer Society)

We’re providing competitive support to help students and recent graduates attend conferences and related events. Up to three (3) recipients may be selected, each receiving up to $500 (USD).

  • Use of funds: conference travel, publication fees, workshops/events.
  • Selection: competitive; awards contingent on available funding.
  • Eligibility: current students & recent alumni; IEEE membership required (provide your member number).
